King's Cup Instructions

Click the "Draw Card" button to draw a card. The card will flip to reveal its value and rule for 5 seconds, now show the other players the card. Follow the King's Cup rules based on the card drawn. Everyone starts out with 10 lives. If a rule forces someone to drink, they tap the "I Drank" button and lose a life. The first person to run out of points downs the King's Cup. The game continues until the players hit the "Go back to Homescreen" button.

King's Cup Cards

Ace

Waterfall: You start drinking and everyone drinks too until you stop.

Two

You: Choose two people to drink.

Three

Me: If you pick this card you drink.

Four

Floor: Everyone has to touch the ground. Last to touch the floor drinks.

Five

Guys: All men drink.

Six

Chicks: All women drink.

Seven

Heaven: All players reach for the sky. Last person to put their hands up drinks.

Eight

Mate: Choose one of the players to be your mate. You and your mate both take drinks.

Nine

Rhyme: Say a word, take turns saying rhyming words with the original without repeats. First person to pause or not rhyme drinks.

Ten

Categories: Take turns naming things within a category you choose. First player to miss has to drink.

Jack

Rule: Create a rule everyone must follow for the rest of the game.

Queen

Question Master: Take turns asking questions to each other quickly. First to pause or laugh has to drink.

King

King's Cup: Add drink to the cup, whoever runs out of lives first downs the cup!
